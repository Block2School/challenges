## The goal of this challenge is to find out the operations you'll have to do

You are given 3 lines:

- On the first line you'll receive:
  - `A` -> An Integer
- On the second line, you'll receive:
  - `B` -> A letter
  - `C` -> A letter
- On the last line you'll receive:
  - `D` -> An Integer
